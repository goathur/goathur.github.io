In collaboration with Shafik Huffman, an industrial designer, we designed a fan that's small enough to fit in one's pocket, but also blows air just as efficiently as other large-sized portable fans. We decided on a constraint of creating this fan with a budget of $100 and a timespan of 5 months.
For this project, I designed the fan blades by doing research on blade aerodynamics and through running simulations to determine the most suitable material for it. I also facilitated in the revisions for the mounting tray, fan case, and electronic orientation, originally designed by my project collaborator.
