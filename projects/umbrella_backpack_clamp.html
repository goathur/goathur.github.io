Umbrella Backpack Clamps

Skills Used

Computer-Aided Design
Drafting
Rapid Prototyping
Dimensioning/Tolerancing

Programs Used

FreeCAD
OrcaSlicer
As a design request from a client I worked with at one of the IDEA Lab makerspaces under the City of San Diego, I designed and 3D printed a set of clamps that can hook an umbrella onto a backpack, so that it can remain open without needing to hold it by hand.
Through collaboration with both my client and one of the IDEA Lab coaches, I created a set of three umbrella clamps that hold onto a backpack strap, based on the thicknesses and widths that vary throughout it.
